About

My name is Joel and I want to become a better developer than I was yesterday.

In 2012, I graduated with a B.A. in English and have since worked an eclectic assortment of jobs (that’s what I get for majoring in English). Long story short, I’ve always tinkered around with web development and landed a position as a junior developer (focus on Wordpress development) for a local startup.

It was love at first site crash.

I started my first dev job with a very basic understanding of development and instantly fell in love with solving the problems that I encountered. Suddenly, my free time began to consist of learning beyond the basics.

Currently, I make a living by freelancing while I continue to embark on my journey to become a better developer. Wordpress development wet my appetite but I’ve since then received an infatuation with Ruby on Rails.

In my spare time I enjoy hammock camping, playing the banjo, and reading Russian literature.
